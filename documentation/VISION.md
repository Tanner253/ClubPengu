## Our Mission

**Waddlebet is not a Club Penguin copy - we're the evolution.**

We've created a Web3-native social gaming metaverse that honors the nostalgia of classic penguin games while transcending into something entirely new. Our platform welcomes all Web3 communities, creating a space where everyone feels at home.

## üéØ Core Strategy

### Why We Keep Penguins & Igloos

- **Nostalgia Capture** - The Club Penguin community is massive and nostalgic. By maintaining the familiar penguin/igloo aesthetic, we capture that original user base
- **Familiar Starting Point** - New players from other communities have a recognizable entry point
- **Respect for Heritage** - We honor what came before while building beyond it

### Why We Add Dogs, Frogs, and More

- **Community Expansion** - Tap into massive, passionate Web3 communities:
  - **Dogs** - The entire Dogecoin ecosystem
  - **Frogs (Pepe)** - The Pepe meme community
  - **Future Communities** - Whales, and more as we grow
- **Cross-Pollination** - Create a melting pot where communities mingle, trade, and play together
- **Web3 Culture** - Embrace the diverse culture that makes Web3 vibrant

### Why This Matters for Survival

- **Traffic is King** - Social platforms need active communities to thrive
- **Multiple Entry Points** - Not everyone loves penguins, but they might love dogs or frogs
- **Network Effects** - The more communities join, the more valuable the platform becomes for everyone
- **Cultural Relevance** - Web3 culture is diverse; we reflect that diversity

## üìù Notes for Developers

### Character Type Implementation

- Character types are stored in the User model as `characterType`
- Space types are dynamically assigned: `igloo`, `doghouse`, `pond`, `apartment`, `lounge`
- Each character type has unique generators in `src/characters/`
- Cosmetics can be character-specific or universal

### Community Events

- Events should celebrate specific communities while welcoming others
- Token-gated events allow communities to create exclusive experiences
- Cross-community tournaments bring everyone together

### Space System

- Spaces are typed but can be customized
- Public spaces welcome all character types
- Private spaces can be token-gated by community or token
- Rental system allows real value exchange
